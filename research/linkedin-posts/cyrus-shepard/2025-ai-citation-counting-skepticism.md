## Summary (paraphrased)

Shepard pushes back hard on a trend he sees among SEO industry voices: as AI
answer engines absorb more informational search traffic, some practitioners
have pivoted to selling "citation counting" (how often a brand's content
gets cited inside AI answers) as the new proof of SEO value — without
demonstrating that citations actually convert to business outcomes the way
clicks once did. He frames this shift skeptically, arguing it risks becoming
a new metric fixation that lets the industry avoid confronting the more
uncomfortable fact that traffic itself, not just its measurement, has
changed. He signals he'll follow up with supporting data ("let's look at the
