## Content

Law describes the Ahrefs blog team working from a shared workspace (he names "Letaido") wired up with access to multiple frontier LLMs, connectors to WordPress/Linear/Slack, Ahrefs' own data, and custom internal apps built without developer or designer involvement. He lists a sample day: reviewing an AI-assisted mockup of a research report, dictating notes into a custom writing tool, reviewing an auto-generated monthly performance report before adding his own comments, checking a "content freshness" dashboard to flag which articles need updates, and scanning a competitor feed tied to keyword data.
