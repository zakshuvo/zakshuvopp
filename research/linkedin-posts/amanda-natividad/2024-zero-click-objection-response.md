## Summary (paraphrased)

The post addresses a common pushback Natividad gets on the zero-click content
framework: if you stop optimizing every post to drive an outbound click, how
do people ever find your website or product? Her argument is that platforms
increasingly reward — and algorithmically favor — content that provides
standalone value without requiring a click, so the goal shifts from
"engineering a click" to "engineering memorability and trust" within the
platform itself. A link, when included at all, becomes a bonus for the
already-convinced reader rather than the primary conversion mechanism.
