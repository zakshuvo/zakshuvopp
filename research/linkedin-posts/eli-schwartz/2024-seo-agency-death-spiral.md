## Content

Schwartz distinguishes SEO-the-discipline (which he says isn't dying) from the SEO agency business model (which he says is in trouble). He describes a "death spiral": agencies cut prices to stay competitive as AI commoditizes basic SEO work, which squeezes margins, overworks teams, degrades results, and drives client churn — repeating. He frames bundling AI services and cutting headcount as reactive, not strategic, and argues clients now scrutinize agency results harder given shrinking organic traffic overall.
