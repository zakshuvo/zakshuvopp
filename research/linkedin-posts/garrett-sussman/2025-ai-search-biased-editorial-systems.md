## Content

Sussman argues the SEO/GEO/AEO/AIO naming debate is mostly a distraction from the real question — whether companies will actually fund organic/AI visibility as the revenue channel it's becoming. His sharper claim: AI search platforms aren't neutral, they're "biased editorial systems" that favor particular brands, sources, entities, and narratives, and marketers need to reverse-engineer those patterns rather than assume the underlying model is impartial.

## Why this post matters for the playbook

Directly relevant to any playbook section on AI-driven distribution/visibility (as opposed to just AI-assisted content production) — argues teams need to treat AI answer engines as editorial gatekeepers with detectable biases, not neutral aggregators. Complements Amanda Natividad's "zero-click" framing and Ross Simmonds' distribution-first approach.
