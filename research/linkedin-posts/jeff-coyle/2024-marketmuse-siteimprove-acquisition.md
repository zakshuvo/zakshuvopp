## Content
Coyle announces that MarketMuse has entered a definitive agreement to be acquired by Siteimprove, framing it as a personal and company milestone (coinciding with Siteimprove's 21st anniversary). He argues the deal lets marketers stop worrying about fragmented tooling for AI-driven search — combining accessibility, SEO, content strategy, and marketing performance analytics into one platform — and frames the acquisition as evidence that content quality and SEO are converging into a single discipline rather than staying siloed. A companion long-form post on the MarketMuse/Siteimprove blog (same day) elaborates: the acquisition is meant to help content teams optimize for both traditional search engines and AI-driven systems like SGE/ChatGPT from ideation through publication, rather than bolting SEO on after content is written.
