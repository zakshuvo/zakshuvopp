## Content

Coyle shares MarketMuse's Q3 2023 State of Generative AI survey results from Content Marketing World: most respondents rated AI-generated content highly, and most companies said they endorse generative AI use in principle — but adoption maturity lagged far behind sentiment. The gaps he flags: most companies have no formal generative-AI policy, very few have done related training, only a small share have rolled tools out org-wide (most usage is still individual/ad hoc), and most allocate no dedicated budget to it at all.

## Why this post matters for the playbook

Useful as a "state of the industry" data point rather than a single practitioner's opinion — shows a gap between positive sentiment toward AI content and actual organizational readiness (policy, training, budget) as of late 2023. Good anchor for a playbook section on why teams need governance/policy work, not just tool access.
