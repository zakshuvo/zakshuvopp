## Summary (paraphrased)

Coyle lays out five shifts he expects to reshape enterprise SEO planning in
2026. The core themes: AI-driven intent mapping is replacing slower, manual
topic-gap analysis; semantic and entity-based optimization is becoming the
baseline rather than a nice-to-have, with strategy shifting from individual
keywords toward topics, entities, and knowledge graphs; technical SEO work
(crawl budgets, audits, fixes) is moving toward CI/CD-style automated
pipelines; content personalization is increasingly built on first-party data
to preserve user trust; and governance — explainable AI models, transparency,
data ethics — is becoming a mandatory part of the SEO/content stack rather
than an afterthought.
